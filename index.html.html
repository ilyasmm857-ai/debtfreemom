The system

How it works

Four clear phases. One small action per day. By the end of the month you will have a real money system in place.

01

Debt Snapshot

List every debt you carry — the balance, interest rate, and minimum payment. No judgment, just clarity. This is the first step most people avoid, and the one that changes everything.

02

Monthly Budget Reset

Build an honest budget for the current month — income first, then essentials, then everything else. The Budget vs. Actual table lets you check in weekly without starting over.

03

Stop the Bleeding Tracker

Identify the spending slipping through unnoticed. Forgotten subscriptions and habits that quietly add up. Most people find money they did not know they were losing.

04

30-Day Action Checklist

One specific, manageable task per day. By day 30 you will have reviewed your debt, built a budget, and tracked your spending — without burning out or giving up.

Why manual entry is intentional

Research consistently shows that writing your own numbers — rather than having them auto-imported — increases financial awareness and follow-through. When you type it yourself, you actually see it. That moment of noticing is the whole point.
